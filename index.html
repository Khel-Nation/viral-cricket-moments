FAQ

Different criteria ke hisaab se answer badal sakta hai. Indian fans ke liye Dhoni ka 2011 World Cup six aur Yuvraj ke six sixes top contenders hain.

Social media ne cricket virality ko kaise change kiya?

Clips instantly global audience tak pahunch jaati hain. Reaction culture aur memes ne virality ko accelerate kiya hai.

Kya future mein aur bhi bade viral moments aayenge?

Almost certainly. Audience size aur digital platforms dono grow kar rahe hain.

Kya controversy wale moments bhi viral hote hain?
